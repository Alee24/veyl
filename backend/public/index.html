✦ Features

Everything private.
Nothing leaked.

Every feature is designed from the ground up to protect your identity — not just your messages.

NEW FEATURE

Disposable Contact Links

Generate a temporary, expiring link to share with anyone — a buyer, a date, a colleague — without revealing your phone number or username. Set an expiry time (15 min to 7 days), a max scan count, and an optional PIN. After use, the link vanishes automatically.

🛡️

End-to-End Encryption

Every message, file, and call is encrypted peer-to-peer. Your conversations belong to you alone — not to any server.

📷

QR Identity Exchange

Add new contacts instantly by scanning a QR code. No phone number lookups, no email needed — pure encrypted identity exchange.

📞

HD Voice & Video Calls

Crystal-clear, low-latency calls over an encrypted peer connection. Works over any internet connection worldwide.

📎

Rich Media Sharing

Share photos, documents and voice notes within encrypted chats. Files are transferred securely end-to-end.

🌐

Offline Nearby Mode

When internet is unavailable, Veyl detects other users on the same local Wi-Fi and switches to offline proximity messaging automatically.
